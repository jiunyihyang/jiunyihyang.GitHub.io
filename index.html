Tsu-Jui (Ray) Fu

I am currently a 2nd-year Ph.D. student at UCSB, advised by William Wang. My research interest lies in natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision (CV). Primarily, I work on language grounding and interacting with environments using language. Besides, I am also interested in information extraction and video analysis. My goal is to bridge the gap between vision and language via the AI system.

I finish my B.S. in National Tsing-Hua University (NTHU) and work with Shan-Hung Wu, Wei-Yun Ma, Chun-Yi Lee, and Hwann-Tzong Chen.
